University Journey

First Day

My first day at university was memorable. I went with my parents. Seniors helped me find my classroom. We completed the CS department form process and later the Head of Department explained the semester system.

Second Day – Orientation

We attended the orientation program arranged by seniors. Teachers gave motivational speeches and seniors arranged activities. We also created a WhatsApp group for class communication.

Life Experience

Midterm Achievement

I scored 38 out of 40 in Programming Fundamentals midterm. This made me very happy and motivated.

Exhibition Experience

After midterms, we participated in an exhibition. We trained a Machine Learning model using a heart attack prediction dataset. Professors asked questions and we presented our project confidently.

Final Exams & Health Issue

During finals, my health became very weak. I had high fever and physical weakness, which affected my preparation. Although my GPA is above 3.5, I am not fully satisfied because of illness.

Family Support

My father supported me a lot. He used to wait outside my department during exams for hours. His support gave me strength.

Travel Experience

I am a day scholar. My university is about 40 minutes outside the city. Daily bus travel is manageable, but sometimes finding transport is difficult. Even a rickshaw can take up to 60 minutes. This experience made me independent.

Personal Growth

These experiences helped me grow academically and personally. I learned confidence, responsibility, patience, and time management.
