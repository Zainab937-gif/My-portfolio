My University Journey

First Day

My first day at university was a memorable experience. I went with my parents. Some seniors were standing there, so I asked them about my classroom and then sat in the class.

After some time, our teacher arrived and announced our roll numbers. He asked us to go to the CS department, get a form from the print shop, fill it out, and submit it to the clerk.

The print shop was crowded, but we finally managed to fill the form and submit it.

Second Day

On the second day we attended an orientation program arranged by seniors in the CS department.

Teachers gave motivational speeches and seniors organized fun games and took pictures.

After the orientation we created a WhatsApp group for our class to share information.
