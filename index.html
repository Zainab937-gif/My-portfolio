My University Journey

Documenting My First Experiences at University

First Day

My first day at university was a memorable experience. I went with my parents. Some seniors guided me to my classroom. Later, our teacher announced roll numbers and asked us to visit the CS department to collect and submit forms. The print shop was crowded, but we managed to complete the process. Our Head of Department then explained the semester system to us.

Second Day

On the second day, we attended an orientation program arranged by seniors. The environment was welcoming and beautifully decorated. Teachers motivated us to work hard and enjoy university life. Seniors organized games and took pictures, making it a memorable event. We also created a WhatsApp group for class communication.
