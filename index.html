My University Journey

First Day

My first day at university was a memorable experience. I went with my parents. Some seniors were standing there, so I asked them about my classroom and then sat in the class.

After some time, our teacher arrived and announced our roll numbers. He asked us to go to the CS department, get a form from the print shop, fill it out, and submit it to the clerk. The print shop was crowded, and it was quite difficult to get the form, but we finally managed to fill it and submit it.

Returning to our department, our Al-Quran teacher had already arrived. We quickly entered the classroom. Later, our Head of Department (HOD) explained the semester system to us.

Second Day

On the second day, we attended an orientation program arranged by the seniors in the CS department. The hall was beautifully decorated and welcoming.

Teachers gave motivational speeches, encouraging us to study hard but also enjoy university life. The seniors organized some fun games and took pictures to capture the memories.

After the orientation, we went back to our department and created a WhatsApp group for our class to share important information. It was a very enjoyable and memorable day.
