About Me

I am a passionate Computer Engineering student who enjoys programming, web development, and learning new technologies. I always try to improve my skills through practice and projects.

My University Journey

First Day at University

My first day was very memorable. I went with my parents. Seniors helped me find my classroom. Later, we completed the form process from the CS department and our Head of Department explained the semester system.

Second Day – Orientation Program

We attended an orientation program arranged by seniors. The environment was welcoming. Teachers gave motivational speeches, and seniors organized activities. We created a class WhatsApp group for communication.

Life Experience

Midterm Achievement

In Programming Fundamentals, I scored 38 out of 40 marks in my midterm exam. I was very happy and motivated by this achievement.

Exhibition Experience

After midterms, we participated in an exhibition. We trained a Machine Learning model using a heart attack prediction dataset. Professors visited our project, asked questions, and we gave a presentation.

Final Exam Challenges

During final exams, my health became weak, which affected my preparation. I experienced fever and physical weakness, but I stayed strong and completed my exams.

Family Support

My father supported me greatly and waited outside during my exams. His support encouraged me during difficult times.

Daily Travel Experience

As a day scholar, I travel daily because my university is outside the city. Sometimes transportation is difficult, but this experience made me independent and responsible.

Personal Growth

All these experiences helped me grow academically and personally. I learned responsibility, confidence, patience, and time management.
